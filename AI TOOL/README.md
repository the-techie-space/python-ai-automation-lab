# 1️⃣ Prompt Engineering (Most Important Concept)

## Why Prompting Matters

Prompting is the most important skill when working with AI.

You are instructing the AI:
- How to behave
- What role to take
- What output format to generate
- What constraints to follow

**Good Prompt → High Quality Response**
